Chocolate Cake

This is a recipe for chocolate cake. Which includes the ingredients needed and how to use them.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Butter and four two 9-inch cake pans.
  3. In large mixing bowl, sift flouwer,sugar and cocoa powder. Add salt,baking powder and baking powder, whisk until everything is blended
  4. In separate bowl, combine milk,eggs,oil and vanilla,and whisk to combine.Then whisky in boiling water.
  5. Add wet ingrediants to dry and stir until combined.
  6. Pour batter into pans and put in oven.
  7. Bake for about 30 minutes.
  8. Let cakes cool for about 10 minutes.
  9. After cakes cool, place one layer on top of the other and layer with frosting.
