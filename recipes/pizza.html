Pizza

This is a recipe on how to make a classic cheese pizza. It shows what ingredients are needed and how to use them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 1 cup of any other shredded cheese
  • can of store-brought pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  • 3 tbps softend unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic minced

Instructions

  1. Combine water and yeast in a large bowl, allow it to rest until yeast forms.
  2. Add salt,flour and 1 tbsp of olive oil, mix well.
  3. Knead the dough for ten minutes.
  4. Form dough into a bowl. Cover dough with rest of olive oil. Cover bowl, and place in warm area to allow dough to rise for about 45 minutes"
  5. Preheat over to 450 degrees F. If you have a pizza pan, lightly oil it with olive oil, if using baking pan use a sheet of parchment paper
  6. Flatten the dough and place it on top of pizza or baking pan.
  7. In mixing bowl, combine butter and garlic and spread it evenly on pizza dough.
  8. Spread the pizza or marinara sauce evenly on the dough.
  9. Spread all the cheese evenly on the dough.
  10. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
